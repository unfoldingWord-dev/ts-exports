A Promise for the Whole World [21-02]

God promised Abraham that through him all people groups of the world would receive a blessing. This blessing would be that the Messiah would come sometime in the future and provide the way of salvation for people from all the people groups of the world.

Translation Notes:

  • through him – That is, “because of one of his descendants.”
  • provide the way of salvation for people - That is, “provide the way for people to obtain salvation” or, “make it possible for people to be saved.”
